Welcome to St. Joseph's Convent

Our Institution, which is 64 years old, is one of the pioneer institution of Ranjhi in the field of Education. The great tradition of the past are maintained and improved upon as the years go by. The top priority of our school is the overall development of the pupil through various activities with special emphasis on moral values and academic excellence.

Due importance to physical development through games and sports is also given. Thus, in keeping with the motto of our school "Virtue Alone Ennobles", every year our institution turns out young citizens of character, balanced in mind , body and spirit-in-services of mankind and the nation.
Education must be a partnership between the school and parents if we are to prepare our children well to meet the challenges and needs of the future. Hence, we need your co-operation, dear parents, in this connection. This website is liason between the parents and the school. Any problem regarding your ward, may be conveyed through this calendar or you may seek an appointment with the Principal. Please co-operate with us and instruct your child to abide by the regulations of the school.

History of Our School
Jesus Christ is the reason of our school and St. Joseph's Convent Sr. Sec. School, is lovingly placed under the protection of St. Joseph, the foster father of Jesus
The school was established in 1957 by the Indian Province of the Sisters of St. Joseph of Chambery as per provisions of constitution of Indian Part III article 30 (i). It is administered by the sisters of Nirmala Province in accordance with general rules governing the above congregation. It is an English Medium Senior Secondary Co-Ed School affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi, Ajmer region. It conduts classes from Primary to Senior Secondary Level.

The school is lovingly dedicated to St. Joseph, the foster father of Jesus Christ with its motto "Virtue Alone Ennobles"

St. Joseph, the Patron of our school, is called a "Just Man" in Holy Scriptures - a man after God's own heart. He was a simple carpenter, hard-working, compassionate and availabe to those in need He was ever ready to serve. He walked Humbly before God.

Every Josephite Teacher and student must emulate St. Joseph by being simple, obidient, hard-working, people of faith and courage to take the place in the world

The school celebrates the feast of its Patron St. Joseph on 19 th March.
